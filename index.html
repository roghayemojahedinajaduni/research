About

Researcher • Marketer • Educator

I am a dedicated researcher with a strong background in marketing management and over ten years of practical experience in sales and digital promotion of health and beauty products. My research focuses on digital consumer behaviour — especially how trust, emotion and cognition shape online purchase decisions in emerging markets.

My MSc thesis, “Examining Consumer Attitudes Toward Affiliate Marketing Among Iranian Online Shoppers”, received a grade of 19/20 and produced two submitted manuscripts. I combine qualitative and quantitative methods (interviews, thematic analysis, and SEM) to develop culturally-informed behavioural models.

Balancing family life as a mother and professional responsibilities has strengthened my resilience, time-management and leadership — qualities I bring to rigorous doctoral research.
